Marketing 1.0

Product-centric marketing where businesses focused on selling what they produced.

Marketing 2.0

Consumer-centric approaches that emphasized understanding customer needs.

Marketing 3.0

Value-driven marketing focusing on emotional connections with consumers.

Marketing 4.0

Emphasizing digital marketing and the power of social media platforms.

Marketing 5.0

Data-driven marketing leveraging analytics and automation tools.

Marketing 6.0

The future of marketing focusing on sustainability and advanced technologies.
